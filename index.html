A lot of words come to mind when watching James Huessy’s comedy-"Intelligent." "High strung." "Why does he smile so much?” James is a distinct personality with nothing to hide, and that upfront approach allows him to paint honest-but-fanciful narratives of his own anxiety. With an innate likability and verbal dexterity to match, he’s a delight on and off stage. And he smiles like that because he’s from Vermont.
